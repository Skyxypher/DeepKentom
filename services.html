Role of AI in Agriculture

Analzing Market Demand.

AI can make crop selection easier and assist farmers in determining which product is the most profitable.

Managing risk.

Forecasting and predictive analytics can help farmers decrease mistakes in business operations and lower the chance of crop failure.
